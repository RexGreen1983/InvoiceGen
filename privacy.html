Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

Information We Don't Collect

InvoiceGen is designed to be completely private:

  • We do not store any of your invoice data
  • We do not require account creation
  • We do not use tracking cookies
  • We do not sell or share any user data

How It Works

All invoice generation happens in your browser. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to download the PDF or share it.
